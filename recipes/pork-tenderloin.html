Pork Tenderloin


This marinated pork loin cooks up nicely on the grill. It tastes best when marinated for 24 hours.

Ingredients

  • Honey
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Brown Sugar
  • Ginger Root
  • Ketchup
  • Fresh Parsley
  • Onion Powder
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Ground Cinnamon
  • Pork Tenderloin

Recipe

  1. Make Marinade
  2. Place pork tenderloins into the bag
  3. Preheat the grill
  4. Remove pork tenderloins from marinade
  5. Cook pork tenderloin
